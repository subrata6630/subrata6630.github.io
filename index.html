Development and Implementation of a Robust Face Detection and Recognition System

Bangladesh University

Abstract:

Face detection and recognition systems have become a cornerstone in modern computer vision applications, ranging from security and surveillance to user authentication and personalized services. This paper presents the design, development, and implementation of a robust face detection and recognition system. The system leverages state-of-the-art deep learning models, including Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and pre-trained architectures like MTCNN (Multi-task Cascaded Convolutional Networks) for face detection and FaceNet for face recognition. The proposed system is evaluated on benchmark datasets, demonstrating high accuracy and robustness under varying lighting conditions, poses, and occlusions. The results highlight the system's potential for real-world applications, with an accuracy of 98.5% on the LFW (Labeled Faces in the Wild) dataset.
